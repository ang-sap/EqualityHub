What is EqualityHub?

EqualityHub is a platform designed to address gender discrimination in schools. Many students experience unfair treatment, stereotypes, and limited opportunities based on their gender. This website provides educational resources, a safe space for discussion, and a secure reporting system to support students and promote inclusivity. Our goal is to create a school environment where everyone feels respected, empowered, and free to reach their full potential.
